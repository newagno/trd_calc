TradingView Guide

EMA (Exponential Moving Average)

TradingView > Indicators > search "exponential"
Select: "Moving Average Exponential" in TECHNICALS
(or from Community: "Exponential Moving Averages" by Merc15)

Settings: Length = 21 (short) or 50 (medium)

How to read:
• Price candles ABOVE EMA line = bullish → "Price above EMA"
• Price candles BELOW EMA line = bearish → "Price below EMA"
• EMA 21 crosses ABOVE EMA 50 = Golden cross
• EMA 21 crosses BELOW EMA 50 = Death cross

MACD

Indicators > search "MACD" > add built-in "MACD"
Settings: default (12, 26, 9)

Look at the histogram (colored bars):
• Bars green / above zero = "Positive (bullish)"
• Bars red / below zero = "Negative (bearish)"
• Blue line crosses ABOVE orange = "Bull crossover"
• Blue line crosses BELOW orange = "Bear crossover"

Bollinger Bands

Indicators > search "BB" or "Bollinger" > add "Bollinger Bands"
Settings: Length = 20, StdDev = 2 (default)

3 lines on chart (upper, middle, lower):
• Price near lower band = potential bounce → "Near lower"
• Price near upper band = potential drop → "Near upper"
• Bands very narrow = squeeze, expect big move → "Squeeze"

Volume

Volume bars shown at bottom of chart by default
Compare current bars to recent average visually
• Shorter than usual = "Below average"
• Normal = "Normal"
• Taller than usual = "Above average"
• One huge bar (2-3x) = "Volume spike"

Funding Rate

NOT on TradingView! Check on:
• coinglass.com > Funding Rate
• Or directly on your exchange

• Around +0.01% = "Positive (normal)"
• Above +0.05% = "Positive (high)" — longs pay more
• Below 0% = "Negative" — shorts pay, LONG better
• Below -0.05% = "Very negative" — strong LONG signal
